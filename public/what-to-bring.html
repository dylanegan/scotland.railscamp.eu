What to bring

In order to maximise Rails Camp Scotland joy, refer to this list when packing your bags.

  • Clothes — be sure to include warm clothes, but also entertain the possibility of warm, sunny weather. It could be 20º and it could be 3º! If we get both, it wouldn't be unusual. A rain jacket and any other wet weather gear could come in handy.
  • Laptop
  • Chargers
  • Toothbrush & Toiletries
  • Towel (no bother if you forget, can be hired for £2 at reception)
  • Medications you take regularly
  • Snacks that you can’t live without
  • Walking shoes or boots if you want to go for an explore
  • Data and files etc that you’d normally rely on getting from the Internet. Rails Camp is a time when bundle install and npm install aren't always possible. Also think about documentation that you may need that's usually accessed online. Dash can be handy here.
  • Camera
