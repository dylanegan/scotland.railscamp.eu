About Rails Camp (Scotland)

Rails Camp first ran in Australia in 2007, organised by the lovely Ben Askins. Since then there has been an event twice a year in Australia. There have also been camps throughout the world, including the UK, but not yet in Scotland.

Rails Camp is a different kind of conference, in that there is no strict schedule (outside of eating times). In fact there is no set agenda. If anything, it's to get people of different backgrounds and experience together, to discuss, hack and experience an event detached from their normal environments. It doesn't even have to be about Rails or Ruby!

We welcome everyone.

Rails Camp Scotland is put together by Dylan Egan and Dylan Fogarty-MacDonald.

Rails Camp Scotland will be a conference that adheres to the Conference Code of Conduct.
