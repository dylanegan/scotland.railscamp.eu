A Technical Retreat in Beautiful Scotland

Rails Camp is a different kind of conference: there is no strict schedule or agenda. Rails Camp aims to create a wonderful environment in which people of different backgrounds and experience can get together, discuss, learn, and hack. Rails Camp is a special experience. It's an event detached from your daily environment.

Please join us for Rails Camp Scotland from September 26 until September 29, 2016, for a few days full of great, like–minded people, inspiring conversations, fun hacking, stunning views, laughs, learnings, and delicious, regional food.

The conference will be held at the lovely Comrie Croft, located in Crieff, just outside of Perth, Scotland. Accommodation will be provided. If you wish to camp, please let us know and we'll coordinate with yourself and the venue to ensure everything is arranged.

Comrie Croft will provide bedding, but please ensure that you bring a towel and bathroom amenities. And don't forget to pack appropriate clothing, as there are lots of walking trails around the venue to enjoy. We'll provide a more complete list of what to bring closer to the date.

We will be arranging a return coach service from Perth train station to Comrie Croft. If you will be making your own way to the venue, please let us know. Public transport is also an option, with a bus service stopping at the venue.

Meals will be provided, but please feel free to bring anything extra. There are fridges available for storage and a kitchen available for you to prepare anything you require.

We Welcome Everyone

Regardless of your skills and background: we want you there. Who you are, what you do and what you bring to the event doesn’t have to be about Rails or even Ruby! Register now and join us in September.

Rails Camp Scotland will be a conference that adheres to the Conference Code of Conduct. Our email is always open and we will provide further contact details leading up to the conference.

Diversity

With the help of our sponsors and supporters we plan to open up a scholarship program to under-represented groups in the community. This program will provide a number of scholarships for individuals to attend Rails Camp Scotland. The scholarship will provide a regular ticket for the conference.

We hope to make an announcement for this program as soon as it is feasibly possible.

About Rails Camp

Rails Camp first ran in Australia in 2007, organised by the lovely Ben Askins. Now, there are camps throughout the world: from the U.S., to Denmark, Poland, New Zealand, and the UK — but not yet in Scotland.

Rails Camp Scotland has been put together by Dylan Egan and Dylan Fogarty–MacDonald. Feel free to say hello, we'd love to hear from you.
