About Rails Camp

Rails Camp first ran in Australia in 2007, organised by the lovely Ben Askins. Now, there are camps throughout the world: from the U.S., to Denmark, Poland, New Zealand, and the UK — but not yet in Scotland.

Rails Camp Scotland is put together by Dylan Egan and Dylan Fogarty–MacDonald.
