The Guide

Welcome to The Guide, where we hope to provide as much information as possible for attendees of Rails Camp Scotland.

Accommodation, Food & Transportation

Accommodation will be provided. If you wish to camp, please let us know and we'll coordinate with yourself and the venue to ensure everything is arranged.

Comrie Croft will provide bedding, but please ensure that you bring a towel and toiletries.

Meals will be provided, but please feel free to bring anything extra. There are fridges available for storage and a kitchen available for you to prepare anything you require.

We will be arranging a return coach service from Perth train station to Comrie Croft. If you will be making your own way to the venue, please let us know. Public transport is also an option, with a bus service stopping at the venue.

Other

Don't forget to pack appropriate clothing, especially as there are plenty of walking trails around the venue to enjoy.

If you are planning on hitting up biking trails and require any assistance please let us know.
